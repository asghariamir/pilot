Learn to analyze and build functions by understanding their behavior: increase/decrease, concavity, jumps and asymptotes.

Increasing & Curving Up

Behavior: • Goes up left→right • Concave up • Gets steeper

Decreasing & Curving Down

Behavior: • Goes down left→right • Concave down • Less steep

Piecewise with Jump

Behavior: • Same x at the break • Open/closed mark value • Two different y-values

S-Shaped (Inflection)

Behavior: • Changes concavity • Inflection • Always increasing

U-Shaped Valley

Behavior: • Decreases then increases • Minimum • Concave up

Approaching a Line

Behavior: • Levels off • Asymptotic to a line • Never reaches it
